WHO AM I?

Hanna Maria Nilsson, that's me. I started the UX-Design program 2020. I will graduate 2022.

I'm a new seed on the fields of UX and I'm ready to grow. What I like especially about UX is that we make "small things" matter and that every step of the UX-process is filled with different kind of techniques and strategies. Creativity is also a big part in UX-thinking and that's one of the best headspaces to be in, if you ask me.

I will never get fully bloomed, there's always more to learn.

This assignment was about making a dating app which focused on people getting to know each other before getting the access to see each others pictures.

At the beginning I had difficulties coming up with ideas, I focused on the problematic areas too much. After some time I got an idea, and that idea lead to another one and after that it all just made sense.

The key was to stop thinking problems and start thinking solutions. That mindset gave me a different kind of energy.

The project was still a challenge but it became an exciting one.
