About me

Hanna Maria Nilsson, that's me. I started the UX-Design program 2020. I will graduate 2022. I'm a new seed on the fields of UX and I'm ready to grow. What I like especially about UX is that we make "small things" matter and that every step of the UX-process is filled with different kind of techniques and strategies. Creativity is also a big part in UX-thinking and that's one of the best headspaces to be in, if you ask me. I'm seeing myself as a little seed with vision of growing until my final days. I will never get fully bloomed, there's always more to learn.

PIXER/Love on a canvas

This assignment was about making a dating app which focused on people getting to know each other before getting the access to see each others pictures. At the beginning I had difficulties coming up with ideas, I focused on all the problematic areas too much. After some time I got one idea, and that one idea lead to another one and after that it all just made sense. The key was also to just stop thinking about the problems and start thinking solutions, it gave me a different kind of energy and the project was still a challenge but it became an exciting one.
